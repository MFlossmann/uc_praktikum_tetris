* Tetris
** Hardware layout
*** Buttons: Shift-Register und PB5, PB6
    - Shift register Buttons:
      | Left | Rotate Counterclockwise | Rotate Clockwise | Right |
      Left + Right : Fast drop
    - PB5: Pause
    - PB6: Reset
** Software layout
*** Playfield: 
     - Bitmap with 40x10 entries (uint16_t[40])
     - Nur 20 Zeilen sichtbar
